Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and associated data for the application.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open your registered email account.
  2. Send an email from the same email address used to sign in to the app.
  3. Send the request to:
    Email: mighty.dev.pro@gmail.com
  4. Use the subject:
    Account Deletion Request
  5. Include your registered email address and a request to delete your account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification of your request, the following data associated with your account will be deleted:

  • User account information stored by the application.
  • User preferences and settings stored by the application.
  • Any application data associated with your account.

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of receiving and verifying the request.

Google Sign-In Users

This application uses Google Sign-In for authentication. Deleting your application account removes data stored by the application. Data stored by Google within your Google Account is managed separately through your Google Account settings.

Contact

For questions regarding account deletion, contact: mighty.dev.pro@gmail.com
